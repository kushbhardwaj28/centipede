Centipede: Arcade shooting game

Controls:

Arrow Keys: To move the ship.

Space: To shoot

P: Start/Pause/Reset

Rules:

-Shoot down the Centipede by hitting on the head else it break into two.

-When its alone its fast.

-If you hit a Centipede, Its game over.

-Its only one chance make it count.

-Hit on a Hurdle count 10 points each.

-Hit on a Centipede count 50 points each.

Play and Enjoy
